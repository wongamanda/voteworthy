    • Why Vote?

    As young people who get to vote in our first election, we appreciate the value of having our voice heard through our voting rights. Voting is an important avenue for change that is only effective if the majority of people take advantage of it.

    Unfortunately, not everyone is going to the polls.

    "During the 2008 presidential election, only 41% of eligible voters making less than $15,000 a year voted, compared to 78% of those making $150,000 a year or more."
